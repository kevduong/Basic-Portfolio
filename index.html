About Me

Kevin Duong is a Southeast Asian do-gooder raised in San Gabriel Valley, but now reps East Los Angeles.


He writes grant checks for nonprofits by day and codes at night. Kevin's daytime job at a family foundation is to provide support and funding to local nonprofits in Los Angeles County.


His passion for coding and technology ignited when he was introduced to Teen Xploring Technology (TxT).


TxT is a nonprofit organization that offers boys of color in South Los Angeles the skills in developing applications in technology for their urban communities.


Inspired by the youths' abilities to lead, manage, and develop products to change their local communnities; Kevin enrolled into one of the top coding institution in Los Angeles -- UCLA Extension's Intensive Coding Bootcamp.


Kevin is a boba and burger aficionado. He also wants a farm of dogs when he retires one day.
